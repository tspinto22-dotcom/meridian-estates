✦ Who We Are

Miami's Most
Trusted Agency

Founded in 2007 by Alexandra Montrose, Meridian Estates was born from a simple belief: that buying or selling a luxury home should be an extraordinary experience — not a stressful one.

Over 18 years, we've grown into Miami's premier luxury real estate firm, with a team of dedicated specialists who live and breathe this market. Our success is measured not in commissions, but in the relationships we build and the lives we help transform.

Born and raised in Miami, Alexandra developed an intimate understanding of the city's neighborhoods, culture, and real estate dynamics from an early age. After a decade working with two of New York's top luxury firms, she returned home to found Meridian Estates.
